Why Use a To-Do List?

A To-Do List is a simple yet powerful tool to organize your tasks, boost productivity, and reduce stress. By breaking down your goals into actionable items, it helps you stay focused, track your progress, and ensure nothing important is missed. Whether for work, personal life, or both, managing your day effectively starts with a good plan.
